Our mission:

Bridging the gender gap in the computer science workforce.

We’ll tackle different computer science topics — game design, web development, artificial intelligence, object oriented programming, just to name a few! Ultimately, projects will be showcased on our website (which we’ll build together). Projects will range from fun video games to AI models that solve contemporary issues - whatever piques your interest!

Web Development + Design

Learn how to design websites and publish them! At the end of the year, you will have a portfolio for all your projects.

Game Design

Program simple games in Pygame (Python library)!

Artificial Intelligence

Learn about neural networks, computer vision, natural language processing... and more!

What are we working on?

Learn about our anticipated and current projects!

Representing History

We are creating video games and webpages to teach others about historical events that we are intrigued by.

Android Video Games
